Rules of the game
Select pairs of pictures to match and select difficulty level.
You are given 3 levels of difficulty 3, 5, 8 seconds to memorize the locations.
You lose when you run out of time. Time is based on the amount of pairs of pictures you have selected.
120 seconds for 8 pictures, 150 seconds for 10 pictures, and 180 seconds for 12 pictures
Press start to play and begins count down.
